Curriculum

8-Week Program, 100% Online with On-Demand Videos and Live Classes.

Grow your skillset by learning coding basics from anywhere!
No time zone or location requirements, 100% online.

🙋‍♀️ Who is this for? This curriculum is great if you want to transition to a career in tech or gain advanced coding skills to help you in your personal or professional life. This package includes two SheCodes workshops - SheCodes Basics and SheCodes Plus. This workshop is designed for complete beginners and could be especially helpful for Product/Project managers, UX/UI designers, Customer support, and QA testers to name a few.
👏 Then what’s next? The world of coding will be demystified. You’ll have advanced coding knowledge and impressive coding projects that are great additions to a developer portfolio. You will also have a better idea of which development path you’d like to pursue. 💪 💻
✔️ After completing this workshop: You’ll gain access to the Pluto's Career Handbook, including email templates and interview tips.
